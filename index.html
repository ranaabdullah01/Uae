UAE Golden Visa

Your pathway to 10-year renewable residency in the UAE

What is the Golden Visa?

The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residence visa valid for 10 years, renewable, and without the need for a sponsor. It offers investors, entrepreneurs, and talented individuals the opportunity to live, work, and study in the UAE.

Property Investment Requirements

  • Minimum investment of AED 2,000,000 in property
  • Property can be off-plan or ready
  • Property must be owned outright (not mortgaged)
  • Valid for the investor, spouse, and children

Golden Visa Benefits

10-Year Residency

Renewable visa for 10 years without a sponsor

Family Inclusion

Includes spouse, children, and parents

Business Opportunities

Easier to start and manage businesses in UAE

Travel Flexibility

Unrestricted travel in and out of UAE
